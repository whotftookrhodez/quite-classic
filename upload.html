how to upload

general:

upload and removal requests must be e-mailed to quiteclassicsite@gmail.com and contain:

- user (if using one e-mail for multiple users) under which to upload

- all needed files (under 100 mb each) and their respective titles (minimum: cover, one or more files of any type)

(requests to an existing user under another e-mail are invalid)

if you haven't uploaded before or are uploading to a new user:

pick a binding e-mail and username, follow the general steps

if uploading to an existing user:

follow the general steps ensuring you're using the binding email for your user

what to expect after submitting:

- a wait time of a day for audio and visual art, a week for any new file type (within reason)

- all uploaded content available publicly to stream and download

- audio converted to flac and mp3 320 formats for download and streaming respectively, cover to png

---

(for edge cases feel free to reach out using another e-mail, however prove your identity and provide a new binding e-mail)
